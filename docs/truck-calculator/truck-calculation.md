# Truck Calculation

## Petrol Engine Trucks

### Last 3 Years [2026, 2025, 2024, 2023]

#### Category 1: Up to 5 ton && up to 2.8L engine
| Tax | Formula |
|-----|---------|
| Customs Duty | (vehicle price + auctionFee) + 15% |
| VAT | (Customs duty + vehicle price + auctionFee) + 20% |
| Environmental Tax | (Customs duty + vehicle price + auctionFee) + 2% |

#### Category 2: From 5 to 20 ton && above 2.8L engine
| Tax | Formula |
|-----|---------|
| Customs Duty | (vehicle price + auctionFee) + 12.5% |
| VAT | (Customs duty + vehicle price + auctionFee) + 20% |
| Environmental Tax | (Customs duty + vehicle price + auctionFee) + 2% |

#### Category 3: Above 20 ton (engine volume does not matter)
| Tax | Formula |
|-----|---------|
| Customs Duty | (vehicle price + auctionFee) + 15% |
| VAT | (Customs duty + vehicle price + auctionFee) + 20% |
| Environmental Tax | (Customs duty + vehicle price + auctionFee) + 2% |

### Above 7 Years Old (Specific Rate & Age-Based Tax)

#### Customs Duty (All Weight Classes)
| Tax | Formula |
|-----|---------|
| Customs Duty | Engine Volume × €1.00 |

#### VAT (All Weight Classes)
| Tax | Formula |
|-----|---------|
| VAT | (Customs duty + vehicle price + auctionFee) × 20% |

#### Environmental Tax (Age-Based)
| Age Range | Years | Formula |
|-----------|-------|---------|
| 7–9 years | 2017–2019 | (Customs duty + vehicle price + auctionFee) × 6% |
| 10–14 years | 2012–2016 | (Customs duty + vehicle price + auctionFee) × 12% |
| 15+ years | 2011 & older | (Customs duty + vehicle price + auctionFee) × 24% |
